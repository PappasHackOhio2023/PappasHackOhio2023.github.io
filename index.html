RecycleSmart

Recycling Made Easy

Challenge Info
VISION

RecycleSmart is a convenient self-scanner provided near recycling bins and locations around campus. The scanner helps students determine what is recyclable and how best to recycle. Our design takes advantage of the barcodes located on the back of every packaged food item to provide information about a given material.

PURPOSE

Our teamâ€™s goal is to promote a sustainable campus culture and encourage smart recycling. According to Waste Management, the average recycling contamination rate, or disposal of trash or recyclables in the wrong recycling bin, is 25%. Our product aims to reduce this contamination rate to 0% by providing an accessible and convenient solution that empowers students to make environmentally conscious decisions and reduce waste on campus.

MORE ABOUT DESIGN

Since not all items have barcodes, our product provides advice through an interactive screen in addition to the scanner. Users can learn about what types of materials are accepted at the specific location.
